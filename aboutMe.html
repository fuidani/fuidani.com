About Me

Daniele is an entrepreneur with a background of Consulting in Transaction Services (M&A) at KPMG Italy and France. Daniele is based in Nairobi, Kenya and is the co-founder of 637 Capital.

637 Capital provides businesses and organizations with digital tools that streamline their workflows and enhance productivity. One of our core offerings is JibuDocs, an AI-enabled Knowledge Management Tool for capturing, organizing and accessing organizational data.

Daniele leads all operations at 637 Capital while also handling all sourcing and management activities. His co-founder in turn handles the technology arm of the company.

We developed JibuDocs from scratch. It now stores one of the largest repositories of (East) African tax and legal documents (approximately 800,000) with exceptional search capabilities. The tool is used by all Big Four in Kenya, Bowmans, and ALN.

We are currently working on our updated version that will be a fully fledged knowledge management and research tool, applicable to all document intense industries.

As part of our consulting efforts, Daniele and his team developed a strong partnership with the world leader in organic macadamia processing, facilitating a loan of EUR 5m and lead the business development of the partner company in the US through 637 Capital’s US entities. Daniele also promoted an ongoing deal in the wind sector securing a LOI of EUR 90 million for a 20% stake and closed its first investment for Savannah Capital.

Prior to being an entrepreneur, Daniele has over 8 years’ experience in Audit and Transaction Services from prior employment at KPMG (5 in Milan, 3 in Paris). Here he served as manager and was responsible for assessing quality of earnings, adjusted net debt, working capital and SPA negotiations for buy and sell-side engagements in deals ranging from €40m to €10b EV. Daniele also gained cross-functional experience in Forensics and Restructuring departments and worked with large corporate and private equity companies in France, Italy, Czech Republic, Brazil, and Portugal.
